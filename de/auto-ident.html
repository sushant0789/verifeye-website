Auto-Ident - KYC in 60 seconds

Onboarding new customers often comes with lengthy registration processes, where the customerhas to fill in lots of personal details. With our Auto-Ident solution, we offer a fast and easy onetouch onboarding process that can be integrated into every application. All your customer has todo is to take a photo of the ID card or passport and make a selfie.
That’s it. The complete verification and onboarding process takes only 60 seconds.

How is it done?

  1. 1 We validate the ID document
  2. 2Then read the information on the ID card or passport
  3. 3When that’s completed, we compare the biometrics from the selfie and the ID document image

What's the cool thing about the Auto-Ident solution?

  • ✓ Fast! KYC in only 60 seconds
  • ✓ Smooth! High quality OCR captures data from ID card -> no need for the customer to type in personal details in a registration form
  • ✓ Automated! No human interaction = no possibility to manipulate data
  • ✓ Can be integrated in every onboarding process
  • ✓ Works perfectly for mobile and desktop solutions
  • ✓ 100% customable -> adapted to your CI
